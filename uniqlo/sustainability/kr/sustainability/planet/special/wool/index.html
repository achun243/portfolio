With UNIQLO’s HeatTech, it is possible to stay warm on cold days while keeping the temperature settings of your thermostat down. We can significantly reduce greenhouse gas emissions if people lowered their room temperature in winter by just a few degrees. UNIQLO strives to make clothes that can allow people to do just that.

>UNIQLO does not source down from farms that practice live plucking. We aim to use traceable down and feathers which have been certified by Responsible Down Standard (RDS) by 2020.
