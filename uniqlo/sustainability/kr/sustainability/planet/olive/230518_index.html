Setouchi Olive Foundation

The Setouchi Olive Foundation is an NPO started by the renowned architect Tadao Ando and lawyer of the Teshima case Kohei Nakabo in response to the Teshima Island incident, one of the worst illegal industrial dumping scandals in Japan’s history. UNIQLO fundraises at our stores to protect and restore the beautiful natural environment of Setouchi.
