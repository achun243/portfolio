TSURUMI
CHILDREN'S
HOSPICE

UNIQLO supports children with incurable illnesses and their families by sponsoring "TSURUMI Children’s Hospice," Japan’s first community hospice. Unlike a hospital, hospices are facilities where children suffering from illness can relax as if they are in their second home. This space has served children and adults in the local area since its opening in Osaka in April 2016.
