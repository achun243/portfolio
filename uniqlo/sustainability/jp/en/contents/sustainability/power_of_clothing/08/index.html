What do you do with clothing
that you no longer need?

UNIQLO’s All-Product Recycling Initiative

It’s a common dilemma: your old clothing no longer fits, or you don’t want to wear the same clothes you wore last year. What do you usually do about old clothing that’s too small, or has been lying around in your closet since your tastes changed?
Whatever you do, don’t throw any clothing away, because it could all still be useful to someone somewhere.
