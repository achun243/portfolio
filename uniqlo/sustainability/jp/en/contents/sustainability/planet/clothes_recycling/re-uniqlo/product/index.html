RE.UNIQLO

UNIQLO
DOWN
RECYCLE

Please bring UNIQLO down product lying in your closet into our stores. We will recycle 100% the down and feathers and give them new life.

Recycling clothes into clothes, UNIQLO’s latest challenge that opens up new links between people, clothing, and the global environment. We are starting with down, which encompasses UNIQLO’s entire down items ranges sold worldwide, and recycling 100% of the down and feathers. Conserving resources, reducing waste, alleviating environmental impact. UNIQLO is rapidly expanding initiatives that target such a future.

*21FW recycled down jacket LCA value for down and feather manufacturing processes (calculated by Toray Industries)

UNIQLO DOWN RECYCLING
Inhouse technology ensures quality

UNIQLO focuses on high quality even in its recycled materials.
Using new technology developed jointly with TORAY, we have managed to efficiently create recycled down as new material.

Recycled Down Jackets
From UNIQLO’s recycled down technology

100% recycled down and feathers

The insulating filling of this jacket is comprised entirely of down and feathers sourced from collected UNIQLO down jackets which are cleaned and carefully recycled.

100% ECONYL® recycled nylon

The fabric of this down jacket is made of ECONYL® recycled nylon produced from pre and post-consumer products such as fishing nets, carpet flooring and industrial plastic from factories.
