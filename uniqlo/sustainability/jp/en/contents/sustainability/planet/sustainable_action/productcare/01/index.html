You can enjoy your clothes longer and benefit our Earth by always taking good care of them. Here are some tips that make garment maintenance easy.

#1 Sustainable things you can do to
make your cotton shirts last longer

Because we want you to enjoy your favorite clothes for longer.
Here are some ideas on how to solve different problems so you can wear your cotton shirts for years to come.

My shirts get creased in the wash so I don’t feel like wearing them.

Have you ever felt you didn’t want to wear your cotton shirts anymore after they get creased in the first wash? We have some tips on how to prevent creases during washing.

  • Reduce spin time

    The regular spin cycle on a washing machine is too long for shirts and that’s why they get so creased. Set your spin cycle to roughly one minute.

  • Stretch out any seams before drying

    Sewn parts of a shirt such as sleeves and buttons tend to shrink in the wash. After the wash cycle is complete, run your hands firmly down any sewn parts in the direction of the seam while the shirt is still damp prior to drying-by machine or hanging.

Ironing shirts is hard work

We all want to wear crisp, smooth cotton shirts, but have you ever felt ironing shirts is time-consuming and tedious? If you know the trick, Here are some tips so that you can reduce ironing time and save electricity.

  • Shake out your shirt to reduce ironing time

    Before placing your shirt on the ironing board, shake and smooth it firmly from top to bottom to remove any large creases and reduce ironing time.

  • Steam forward, slide back

    When moving your iron forward and back, release steam on the forward movement to soften the fibers, and turn off the steam on the way back for a firm press, and an efficient, crisp finish.

Reducing ironing time by one minute can save 20Wh of electricity*. You try to reduce environmental impact when using your cotton shirts. So UNIQLO takes extra care to reduce environmental impact in the manufacturing process.
