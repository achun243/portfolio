Doraemon has joined UNIQLO as a global sustainability ambassador. Doraemon is here to show us how sustainable steps can be our secret gadgets to help transform the world into a better place for all. At UNIQLO, we strive to do this through the power of clothing. Doraemon’s pledge to help create a much brighter future is reflected in his change from his iconic blue to green. Are you ready to join Doraemon and UNIQLO in going green? Let’s switch into sustainability mode together!
