Sustainability
Report 2022

The world is still in chaos. But we are seeing clear, fresh signs of improvement. Our ultimate aim is to change the world for the better by making and selling great clothes. To achieve that, the entire Fast Retailing Group is working together on transformative innovation. Building a sustainable supply chain that helps create a new LifeWear industry. Creating an organization that respects diversity, and contributing to society. Making clothes that anyone can wear comfortably every day. Our many initiatives designed to create a good future for our planet, society, and people are introduced in one neat report. The Fast Retailing Sustainability Report's website is updated once a year.
