THE POWER OF CLOTHING by Ayumu Hirano

Dreams, challenges, and joy

UNIQLO Global Brand Ambassador and professional snowboarder Ayumu Hirano made an appearance at the "LifeWear Day 2023 with Ayumu Hirano”, held on his home slopes. He demonstrated his world-class technique in front of many children who admire him. During the talk session, he also spoke sincerely about the importance of going all out to achieve your dreams and overcoming obstacles. He also discussed "climate change," which affects snow, an essential element of snowboarding.

Adam Scott in Sustainability Mode

Professionalism is about humanity

UNIQLO Global Brand Ambassador and professional golfer Adam Scott recently made an appearance at an event aiming to educate the next generation. Not only did he demonstrated world-class techniques to the young golfers with hopes of eventually going pro, but he also advised the importance of personal development with them based on his own experiences.
The second half of the session turned to studying UNIQLO’s latest initiatives, including the manufacture of clothing using recycled PET bottles. Participants also considered the future of clothing.

Shingo Kunieda in Sustainability Mode

What is “SPOGOMI”?

At the UNIQLO Asakusa Store professional wheelchair tennis player Shingo Kunieda participated in “SPOGOMI,” an event that turns garbage collection into a sport-like activity and everyone can enjoy while wearing matching clothes. Kunieda was completely drawn into the fun. As we look out over our clean streets, let’s think about the significance of the initiative and what we can do today for the future of the earth.
