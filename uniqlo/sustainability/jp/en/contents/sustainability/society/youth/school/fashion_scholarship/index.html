Global Graduate Fashion Scholarship

The Fast Retailing Foundation established the Global Graduate Fashion Scholarship (GGFS) .
It aims to help inspired and talented students study at world-class fashion graduate schools and contribute to the development of a better society through the power of fashion and design.

The GGFS seeks to support students who have the enthusiasm and aspiration to better our society by sharing the knowledge and skills they acquire through the program.
We look for students who pursue knowledge and expertise not for its own sake, but for how that knowledge can connect and support a more integrated, future-oriented approach to solving real world problems and developing a better society.

We expect that the GGFS scholars will emerge as the next generation of leaders by providing them with the best possible environment for study and the experience of learning from professors together with a diverse and talented group of students from around the world.

Outline of the Program

Number of applicants
Up to 10 people
Target graduate schools
UK／Royal College of Art
US／Parsons School of Design, Rhode Island School of Design, Fashion Institute of Technology
Qualifications
Those who are accepted and enroll in the master's program of the target graduate school in September 2022
Scholarship Benefit
The scholarship amount will cover these three fees which are required by the schools
1) Tuition fee for the degree program period (up to 2 years)
2) Health insurance fee
3) Mandatory fees for enrollment

<Maximum amount>
UK £ 66,000
US $ 132,000
Selection schedule
1) Application deadline: February 25, 2022
2) Application and document review: March 2022
3) Interview selection: April 2022
4) Final decision: by end of April 2022
