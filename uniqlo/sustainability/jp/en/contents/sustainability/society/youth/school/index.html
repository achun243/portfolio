Preparing young people to become tomorrow’s leaders is pivotal to UNIQLO’s mission of Unlocking the Power of Clothing. We maintain many programs to that end. One is Power of Clothing Project, through which our personnel visit schools around Japan to teach about clothes recycling. Other programs include providing work experience for children at our stores and fostering their interest in art and sport. We also leverage our worldwide network to provide learning opportunities so children can fulfill their potential.
