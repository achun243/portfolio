Scholarship Program for Japanese Students at Harvard Business School

The Fast Retailing Foundation supports students who want pursue areas of study that can contribute to educating about and solving social problems by acquiring advanced knowledge on a global level through a world-class education at HBS.
We expect that the scholars will emerge as the next generation of leaders by providing them with the best possible environment for study and the experience of learning from professors together with a diverse and talented group of students from around the world.

2021 Scholarship Overview

Annual Recipient
One
Scholarship Benefit
Total $200,000 per recipient (over two years)
Eligibility requirements
  • Those who want to pursue fields that contribute to educating about and solving social problems.
  • Applicants must be Japanese citizens throughout the enrollment period.
  • Applicants preferably will not receive any other Japan-based scholarship grants. However, scholarships with repayment obligations in Japan and abroad, non-Japanese scholarships, and non-tuition/living expense grants such as research grants, entrepreneurship funds, temporary grants from the government, etc., can be combined with this scholarship.
  • Applicants that have been accepted and will enroll in HBS in September 2021.
Application Process
The following documents must be submitted by e-mail by the application deadline
  • Resume (Free Format)
  • Application (Specified Format*)

Document screening

Final decision
By the end of April 2021

Important Notice

  • This scholarship is for privately funded international students and cannot be combined with other scholarships or company dispatch programs.
  • From the fall of 2021, the recipients of this scholarship may change from the Fast Retailing Foundation, but the total amount of the scholarship will not change over the two-year benefit period.
