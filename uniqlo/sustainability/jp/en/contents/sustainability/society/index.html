Social Business

Poverty, economic disparities, and other social ills have unfortunately accompanied strong economic development in emerging nations. The Grameen UNIQLO brand in Bangladesh contributes to safe and secure working conditions, fosters the local textile industry’s development, and assists the needy.

  • GRAMEEN UNIQLO (Bangladesh)

Disaster Relief

We leverage our store network around Japan to provide clothing and financial assistance following disasters. We provided aid worth 3.3 billion yen after the Great East Japan Earthquake in 2011. In the following year, we launched the Reconstruction Support Project to assist nongovernment organizations and open stores in affected areas. We have provided disaster relief around the world, including after Hurricane Sandy in the United States in 2012, after the Sichuan Earthquake in China and Typhoon Haiyan in the Philippines in 2013, and after the Hualien Earthquake in East Taiwan in 2018.
