CLOTHING
SUPPORT
THROUGH RE.UNIQLO

Through theRE.UNIQLOproject, we collect lightly used clothing from customers and provide them to refugees and others experiencing adversity. We collaborateclosely with the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees(UNHCR)and nonprofit and nongovernment organizations around the globe to scrutinize shipments and delivery routes to ensure that people get what they need.
