2015.01
PASSING ON LOVE THROUGH
THE POWER OF CLOTHING
~recycled clothing donation in Ludian county~

The 2014 Ludian earthquake struck Ludian County, Yunnan, China, with a magnitude of 6.1 on 3 August. The earthquake killed at least 617 people, and injured 3,000 more. Over 12,000 houses collapsed and 30,000 were damaged.

This report focuses on the recycled clothing donation to Ludian County. We passed on our love to the disaster victims through the power of clothing.
