UNIQLO WHEELCHAIR
TENNIS TOUR

The UNIQLO Wheelchair Tennis Tour is one of the largest international competition programs in parasports, which boasts over 160 tournaments across 40 different countries in every region of the world. UNIQLO has been the title sponsor of the tour since 2014 and provides uniforms for staff, including ball kids and chair umpires.
