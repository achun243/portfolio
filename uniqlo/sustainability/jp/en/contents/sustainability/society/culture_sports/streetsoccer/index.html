USA

Street Soccer USA is a non-profit organization that offers soccer-based life skills program for youth living in poverty to experience the joy of sports. UNIQLO US is an official apparel sponsor of Street Soccer USA. Our stores also host Street Soccer teams in our work experience program and hired several people. These efforts were recognized by the ESPN Sports Humanitarian Award (Corporate Community Impact Award) in July 2018.
